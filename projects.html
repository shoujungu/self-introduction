Impact Factor Prediction

In this project, I built a model to predict the journal's impact factor range that a manuscript will be published, based on the abstract of the manuscript. The training data were obtained from Pubmed database. Raw MEDLINE file was parsed and cleaned by Python and Pandas . Natural language was processed by spaCy. Word2vec was performed by Scikit-Learn. Final model was trained by Lightning. Detailed codes can be found here.



2017 Publications Analysis

In this project, I analysed all 1,292,498 publications collected by Pubmed database in 2017. The analysis including:

  • Publication count ranking by journal & Title keyword ranking;
  • Most studied tissue and diseases;
  • comparison of publications between Chinese and non-Chinese principal investigator;

Tools used in the analysis are Python, Pandas and Matplotlib. Detailed codes and figures can be found here.
